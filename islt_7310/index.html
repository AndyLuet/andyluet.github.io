Hello! I'm Andrew. I'm a UX/UI Designer and Illustrator with a passion for clean, minimalistic design.

Goals

My goal for this website is to showcase my skills as a designer to help aid me in searching for jobs. I want to show potential employers what I am capable of and why they should hire me. I hope to be able to showcase some of the previous UX/UI design work I have done, as well as some illustrations I have made. I would like there to be a unique "about me" section showcasing things that I am interested in.
