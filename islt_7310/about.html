About me

Hello, I'm Andrew. UX/UI Design is my passion and a skill I am constantly improving. I am a creative UX designer with 1 year of experience facilitating and supporting human interactions with complex systems and software in the professional world. I also have 3 years of freelance work experience, communicating with clients to achieve their desired look while maintaining high usability. This is paired with many years of training and education. I maintain user-centric designs while adhering to prescribed interface principals and related development goals. I am dedicated to creating inviting and unintimidating interfaces for everything.
