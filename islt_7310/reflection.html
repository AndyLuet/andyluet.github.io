Personal Reflection

I started working with design back in 2014 when I was first introduced to programs such as Photoshop and Illustrator. I went to a high school called RockBridge here in Columbia, MO. It was at this school that I met a teacher who would be very influential in my life and career choice. Patrick Sasser taught a Web Design class which I took in 2016. This class taught me the very basics of web design and why it is important. I instantly fell in love with web design. It allowed me to take my love for design and share it with thousands of people around the world through the web. I wanted to create beautiful sites where people would enjoy their experience.

The rest of high school and most of college, I was either taking classes on design, or I was doing freelance work to help build my name and experience. Creating small graphics for streamers (twitch.tv) and small websites for free for local businesses were just a few of the ways I built my experience during my free time in college. In 2021, I applied and was accepted to be a UX/UI Designer at MidwayUSA, which was a huge step in my career as a web designer. Late last year, I was given an offer by MidwayUSA to work full time for the company when I graduate from the University of Missouri in May of 2022.

My career goals are to eventually run my own design company. I want to take contract work from clients around the world and have complete control over the future design of the web. This is something that I have wanted for as long as I can remember, and I hope to make it come true in the near future.

Summary

Overall, I feel as though my career is just starting. I hope to have many more memorable work experiences in the future. I also hope to advance as a designer and illustrator, honing my skills and gaining enough experience to land a job anywhere I apply. My ultimate goal for my career is to own my own tech-based company. I have no idea what this company will do or what it will be named, but I hope to do this in the future.
